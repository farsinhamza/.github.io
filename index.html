ABOUT AQUARIUM

CISO Institute of Oceanic Studies Pvt Ltd

India’s Largest Public Aquarium ” Marine world” is home to a variety of small to large magnificent aquatic species accommodated in life-sized tanks and overhead aquarium. Ciso Institute Of Oceanic Studies Private Limited is a techno commercial venture started in 2007 by a team of expat businessmen and engineers dedicated to the popularization and awareness creation of the knowledge related to oceans, marine & freshwater animals.
